Earth Defense -- version history

v2.0.0

  • Isometric rendering: diamond tiles replace top-down grid
  • Elevation system: terrain height affects range, speed, LOS, and cost
  • 15 levels across 3 themed worlds: Desert Outpost, Urban Ruins, Alien Terrain
  • Cliff face rendering between elevation transitions
  • Camera panning: WASD/arrows, mouse drag, and scroll wheel
  • Elevation-bucketed depth sorting for correct painter ordering
  • Bresenham line-of-sight: towers blocked by tall terrain
  • Slope speed modifiers: enemies slow uphill, speed downhill
  • Tower cost multiplier on elevated build slots (+25% per level)
  • Ellipse range indicators for isometric perspective
  • World-themed color palettes and tile textures
  • Economy rebalance: starting credits 200 to 150, passive income 5 to 2
  • Tower rebalance: adjusted costs, damage, and sell refund (50% to 40%)
  • World-grouped level selector in menu
  • HUD shows world name, TowerPicker shows elevation costs, TowerInfoPanel shows effective range

v1.0.1

  • Added link preview image (Open Graph / Twitter Card)
  • Added player guide and changelog pages
  • Level indicator in HUD top bar and level-complete overlay
  • Cloudflare cache purge on deploy

v1.0.0

  • Initial public release
  • 5 levels across desert, coastal, mountain, urban, and mothership themes
  • 5 tower types: Laser, Missile, Cryo, Rail Gun, Pulse Cannon
  • 5 enemy types: Drone, Skitter, Brute, Shielded, Swarm Cluster
  • Tower upgrade system with kill requirements (3 kills for tier 2, 5 for tier 3)
  • Player guide with tower and alien reference
