Aliens

Name Description
● Drone Basic scout. Average speed, low HP.
● Skitter Very fast, very fragile. Rushes past slow towers.
● Brute Extremely tough and slow. Needs heavy firepower.
● Shielded Energy barrier absorbs 50% damage. Shield regenerates.
● Swarm Cluster Splits into 3 mini-drones on death. Kill the children too.

Towers

Name Description Cost
▲ Laser Turret Fast fire rate, low damage per shot. Good all-rounder. $50
▲ Missile Battery Slow but devastating. Splash damage hits groups. $75
▲ Cryo Emitter Slows all nearby enemies. Low damage, high utility. $60
▲ Rail Gun Extreme range and damage. Very slow fire rate. $100
▲ Pulse Cannon Shockwave hits everything in radius. Expensive. $150

Tips

Click a build slot to place a tower. Click a placed tower to upgrade or sell it. Towers need kills before they can upgrade (3 for tier 2, 5 for tier 3). Cryo + Missile is a strong combo against groups. Save credits for later waves -- they get harder.
